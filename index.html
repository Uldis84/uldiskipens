Frequently asked.

Everything you need to know before working together.

What type of content do you specialise in?+
I specialise in long-form YouTube content, primarily food, travel, and lifestyle. Videos that need strong narrative structure, good pacing, and retention-focused editing.
What's your turnaround time?+
For a typical 10–20 minute YouTube video I deliver a first cut within 5–7 days. Rush delivery is available, just reach out.
Which software do you use?+
Adobe Premiere Pro for editing, After Effects for motion graphics and titles. I deliver in any format. Optimised for YouTube or raw ProRes.
How many revisions are included?+
2 rounds of revisions included as standard. Everything else are minor extra costs.
What do you need from me to get started?+
Raw footage, reference videos, a brief outline of the story, and your music preferences. That's it. I handle the rest.
